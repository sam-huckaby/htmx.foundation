Who are we?

In the dynamic world of web development, the HTMX Foundation, a bastion for promoting and defending hypermedia across the internet, faced a pivotal moment. The foundation, once a small collective of enthusiastic developers, had grown exponentially, mirroring the evolving complexity and reach of hypermedia. However, with this growth came challenges. The landscape of web development was shifting rapidly, with new technologies emerging and older ones falling out of favor. The foundation needed a visionary leader to navigate these turbulent waters and ensure the continued relevance and advancement of hypermedia technologies.

Enter Teej_dv, a seasoned software developer known for his innovative approach and deep understanding of the web. Recognizing the foundation's potential and the critical need for strong leadership, Teej_dv stepped in as chairman. His mission was clear: to rejuvenate the foundation, foster community engagement, and champion the development of cutting-edge hypermedia technologies.

Amidst the sweeping changes at the HTMX Foundation, OCamlist.com the premier online destination for OCaml-themed apparel, has stepped up as the first official sponsor of the foundation.

Through Teej_dv's leadership and Ocamlist.com's unique support, the HTMX Foundation is embarking on a new chapter, poised to defend and advance hypermedia in the ever-evolving digital landscape, with a hint of humor to keep things interesting.
